Tortilla with Quesillo

Ingredients

  • 6 tortilla
  • 1/2 cup of cheese
  • vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 cup chopped cabbage
  • 2 lemons
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • salt and pepper

    • Directions

    1. For the cabbage salad, mix the chopped cabbage with the lemon juice, salt and pepper to taste.
    2. Place a little cheese on one tortilla and cover with the other, fry in a pan with vegetable oil until the tortilla turns golden brown.
    3. Serve the tortilla with cheese, add the cabbage salad on top and place the tomato sauce on top.
